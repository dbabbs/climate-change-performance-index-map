Climate Change Performance Index 2020

The 2020 Climate Change Performance Index map shows the countries with the highest achievements in climate protection. The CCPI includes 57 selected countries and assesses countries' performance in four categories: GHG Emissions, Renewable Energy, Energy Use, Climate Policy.

Based on the 2020 Climate Change Performance Index, Sweden was ranked as the country with the highest achievement in climate protection. In this year, no country has achieved enough to be placed into the top three.

Source: Climate Change Performance Index Report 2020.
